UMak Dance Xtreme

The Umak Dance Extreme (UDX) is a modern hip hop dance group in the University of Makati. For the past ten years, the group performed in various programs in the university and competition within Metro Mala. The group won in many contest and eventually put their name in the limelight. They proved their worth as legitimate artist in Umak. All members embrace the responsibilities and obligations of an artist in helping the university in pursuing the discipline, integrity and excellence. The group believes that dance is not just an outlet or a hobby but a lifestyle. The group is under the supervision of the Center for the Culture and the Arts (CCA).

Who can Join?

UMak students who are interested and want to enhance their talent in Music and in performing.
