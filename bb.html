UMak Brass Band

The pioneer members of the Umak Brass band during their rehearsal and recording of the ALCU Hymn last November 2011. The brass band acquired their first instruments through the support of President Tomas B. Lopez, Supreme Student Government (SSG), PESCO, Umak Educational Development Foundation, Inc. (UMEDFI), and the Committee for Culture and the Arts (CCA). The band is being conducted by a veteran band conductor Mr. Isaac Cariaga, while Prof. Stanley Rovira serves as adviser.

Who can Join?

UMak students who are interested and want to enhance their talent in Music and in performing.


Requirements

  • Must have Music instrument esp. wind instruments

  • Know how to read Music notes

  • Develop self esteem and confidence to perform over crowd.
