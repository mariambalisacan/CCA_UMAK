UMak Jammers

Umak Jammers is composed of talented musicians which aims to show case their skills in order to entertains not only students but also the faculty and staffs of the university -Perform to inspire.


Vision
"To discover hidden talents of Umakians in terms of being musicians."

Mission
"To produce quality musicians that can complete in the music industry."

Who can Join?

UMak students who are interested and want to enhance their talent in Music and in performing.

Requirements

  • Must be bonified of Umak

  • No age limit

  • Knows how to play instrument (e.g. drums, guitar, keyboard/piano)

  • Vocals: know how to sing (variaties)

  • Willing to learn

  • Dedicated & passionate musician


    • Benefits

  • Scholarship (full or half ) it depends on how you perform and how often you attend in the practice.

  • Enhance more of your talent

  • Develop self esteem and confidence to perform over crowd.

  • Meeting new people with the same interest

    • Practice

  • Monday & Wednesday 1:00pm-5:00pm Admin Bldg. 6M
