About CCA

    Local Government Code of 1991 “Within their respective territorial jurisdiction, local government units shall ensure and support among other things, the preservation and enrichment of culture.” (Chapter II. Sec. 16)


    DILG Memorandum Circular 2002-81 All provinces, cities and municipalities through their respective sanggunians are encouraged to create the Provincial, City/Municipal Councils for the Culture and the Arts chaired by the local Chief Executives. is a project of the Office of the President under the supervision of the Office of the Vice President for Administration. The UMak-CAC’s focuses on the cultivation and promotion of the excellence through arts and culture. The center ensures equal opportunity for participation of all cultural organizations and artistic group in the university in the year round artistic activities. The center’s objectives are anchored on the Committee on the Culture and Arts thrust on upliftment and enrichment of Makati Culture. It shall be the commitment of the UMak-CCA to support the cultural organization and other artistic group in the university and in and outside of Makati.

Organized in 2010, the University of Makati Committee for Culture and the Arts (CCA) is tasked to spearhead initiatives for the development of arts and culture in the university. It is comprise of ten members from the university's academic and administrative department. We stage cultural and arts related events (e.g. stage plays, art exhibits and competitions, concerts etc.) We also organize academic activities (e.g. symposia/fora, seminars, workshops and trainings) that promote the understanding and appreciation of arts and culture.


Vision

“ The University of Makati Center for Culture and the Arts (UMAK-CCA) envisions a vibrant cultural activity of the various cultural organization and artistic groups in the university in promoting the culture of excellence. ”



Mission

“ To promote the cultural excellence in the university, the UMak- Center for Culture and Arts together with the various cultural organizations and artistic group will develop creative artistic performances that promote the ideals of the true Makateno and citizens of Makati. ”


Objectives

  • To develop and promote arts and culture using all forms of creative expression.

  • To develop sustainable partnership with organization, association, foundation and businesses in the field of art and culture.

  • To foster standards and ideals of distinction in the arts.

  • To broaden access and participation of cultural organizations and artistic groups in various cultural events in and outside Makati.

  • To support cultural workers and artists and art organizations in the university in fulfilling their creative task.

  • To encourage a collaborative work of cultural organizations and artistic groups in any university wide activity.
